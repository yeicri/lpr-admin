How It Works

Our Rescue Process

A clear, proven 5-step process to get your project back on track.

1

Submit Your Case

Fill out our form or call us. We need your permit number, address, and a description of the issue.

2

Diagnostic Review

We pull your full permit history from ePlan and CityView and identify every outstanding comment or violation.

3

Action Plan

You receive a written summary of findings, a proposed scope of work, and a fixed-price quote within 24–48 hours.

4

Rescue Execution

We prepare correction packages, coordinate with engineers and inspectors, and submit all required documentation.

5

Permit Closed

We track every inspection, confirm approval, and deliver final confirmation that your permit is closed and your project is clear.
